Arabian horse

The Arabian horse is renowned for its beauty, endurance, and intelligence. Originating from the Arabian Peninsula, this breed is one of the oldest and purest in the world. Characterized by its distinctive head shape, high tail carriage, and refined appearance, the Arabian horse is not only a symbol of elegance but also of strength and stamina. These horses are known for their versatility and are often seen excelling in endurance riding, show jumping, and dressage. Their spirited yet gentle nature makes them a favorite among equestrians globally.

  • Name: Arabian horse
  • Lifecycle: 25-30 years
  • Eat: Oats
